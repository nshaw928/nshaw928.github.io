About me

Hi, I'm Nick.

I am a medical sciences student at the University of Cincinnati. Academically, I am interested in molecular and structural biology. I love the academic research environment, and because of this, I, naturally, spend a lot of time in the research lab I am a part of (seegarlab.com). In the future, I am planning on pursing a PhD in the biomedical sciences, and I hope to be able to make a difference by solving protein structures and discovering novel protein interactions.

Outside of academics, I enjoy a dynamic and exciting life. I enjoy going to local coffee shops, hiking and rock climbing, and fermenting my own foods.

I am a strong strategic thinker and planner. I also excel in idea creation and execution. I value excellence, ambition, and education.
